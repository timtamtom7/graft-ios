# Graft — App Store Assets

## App Icon Concept

**Visual:** A stylized hourglass composed of two angled parallel lines forming an abstract X, with a small hammer icon at the center intersection — representing the intersection of time and effort (craft).

**Alternative concept:** A simple carpenter's square (set square) with a practice dot in the lower-right quadrant, on a dark background.

**Color:** Purple accent (#e879f9) on near-black (#0d0d0e)

**Reference design language:** The icon should feel like it belongs next to Field Notes or a machinist's logbook. Not flashy. Purposeful.

**Elements to avoid:** Stars, trophies, fire streaks, thumbs up, generic checkmarks. No gradients that scream "productivity app."

## App Description

```
Graft is a practice tracker for people who are learning something real.

You pick one skill. You practice. You log it. At the end of the month: total hours, practice days, and a simple view of your week. No leaderboards. No social features. No streaks that punish you for taking a sick day.

Just you, your skill, and the honest record of the time you put in.

— What you get —
• Log sessions in under 10 seconds (duration, how it felt, optional notes)
• A clean monthly heatmap showing every practice day
• Weekly progress dots so you can see your rhythm at a glance
• Up to 1 year of history on the Track plan
• Multiple skills on Master

— Who it's for —
The guitarist who practices scales alone in their room. The self-taught developer building projects at night. The language learner using Anki and talking to themselves. People who know that mastery is a long game — and who want a simple log to show them how far they've come.

— No fluff —
Graft doesn't send you motivational notifications. It doesn't tell you to maintain a streak or punish you for missing a day. It just tracks what you did.

Graft: Put in the work.
```
